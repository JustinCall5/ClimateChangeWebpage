WAYS TO HELP STOP CLIMATE CHANGE!



There are many ways to help prevent climate change,for those of you who dont know how heres a list of simple things you can do to help!



1. Use energy wisely — and save money too!,

Swap your gas stove for an electric stove, which will also lower indoor air pollution Unplug computers, TVs and other electronics when you’re not using them



2. Green your commute

Take public transportation such as Buses and trains, or just simply walk or bike.



3. Eat for a climate-stable planet

The decisions we make about food can have a profound effect on the environment. Buy organic and local when able, eat more meat less meals



4. Get charged up with renewables

The global push for cleaner, healthier energy is on. With costs dropping every day, renewable energy is the best choice for the environment and the economy.




Although these steps are very easy/simple it will have a positive impact on earth and will be beneficial in the long run.
